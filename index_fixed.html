Welcome to HIBIAFRICA

Raising Spirit-filled, biblically grounded, and mission-oriented leaders across Africa and beyond.

🕊️ About HIBIAFRICA Directorate

Harvestime International Bible Institute (HIBI) – African Directorate serves as the continental administrative and academic headquarters of Harvestime International Network USA across Africa.

👑 Our Founders

Founded by the late Dr. Argis Hulsey and Dr. (Mrs.) Patricia Hulsey to train labourers for the global harvest.

📜 Vision & Mission

Our vision is to raise biblically trained, Spirit-filled, and mission-driven leaders who will transform their world.

🎓 Programs Available

Certificate, Diploma, Bachelor, PGD, Master, and PhD programs offered both online and onsite.

🌍 Leadership

Under the leadership of Dr. Samuel Abiodun Oladimeji and a team of seasoned continental officers.

🌐 National Chapter Directors

HIBIAFRICA operates across several nations, both Anglophone and Francophone, under one vision.

🏫 Admissions

Enrollment is open to ministers, leaders, and believers seeking theological and ministerial growth.

📰 News & Updates

Stay tuned for the latest updates from HIBIAFRICA including convocation, DAFAD, and leadership events.
