About HIBIAFRICA

Established as the continental extension of Harvestime International Network (USA), HIBIAFRICA coordinates national chapters, partner ministries, and students across Anglophone and Francophone Africa.

Identity & Mandate

Introduced to Nigeria by Dr. Samuel Abiodun Oladimeji in 2007/2008 and later mandated to spread the vision across Africa. Head Office (Africa): Osogbo, Osun State, Nigeria.

Global Covering

HIBIAFRICA functions under the oversight of the Founder and Worldwide President of Harvestime International Network (USA), ensuring doctrinal alignment and international ministry standards.
