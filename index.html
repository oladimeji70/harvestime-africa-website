About HIBIAFRICA

HIBIAFRICA serves as the continental administrative and academic headquarters of Harvestime International Network USA across Africa, committed to raising Spirit-filled, biblically grounded, and mission-oriented leaders for the global harvest.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To raise, equip and release Christ-like leaders who will advance the Kingdom of God across Africa and beyond.

Mission: Providing holistic, affordable and accessible biblical and ministerial education, mentoring Christian leaders in character, competence and calling.
