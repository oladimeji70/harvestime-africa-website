Welcome to HIBIAFRICA

Harvestime International Bible Institute Africa (HIBIAFRICA) is the Continental Africa Directorate of Harvestime International Network (USA). We train and equip biblical, Spirit-filled, and mission-minded leaders for the worldwide spiritual harvest across Anglophone and Francophone Africa.

Our Mandate

Since 2007, under the leadership of Dr. Samuel Abiodun Oladimeji, HIBIAFRICA has expanded its vision throughout the continent with academic excellence, community transformation, and kingdom leadership training.
