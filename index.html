🕊️ About HIBIAFRICA Directorate

Harvestime International Bible Institute (HIBI) – African Directorate serves as the continental administrative and academic headquarters of Harvestime International Network USA across Africa.

We are committed to raising Spirit-filled, biblically grounded, and mission-oriented leaders for the end-time harvest across both Anglophone and Francophone Africa.

👑 Founders

Founded by the late Dr. Argis Hulsey and Dr. (Mrs.) Patricia Hulsey to train labourers for the global harvest.
