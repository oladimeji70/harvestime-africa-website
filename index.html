🕊️ About HIBIAFRICA Directorate

Harvestime International Bible Institute (HIBI) – African Directorate is the continental administrative and academic headquarters for Africa. We coordinate both Anglophone and Francophone nations under one divine mandate: to train and equip labourers for worldwide spiritual harvest.

We operate in Nigeria, South Africa, Benin, Cameroon, Niger, Burkina Faso, Togo, Mali, Chad, Ghana, Côte d’Ivoire and other nations through Bible colleges, leadership institutes, refresher courses and ministerial mentorship.

👑 Our Founders

HIBI (USA) was founded by the late Dr. Argis Hulsey and his wife, Dr. (Mrs.) Patricia Hulsey, with a burden to raise labourers for the global harvest.

  • Dr. Argis Hulsey (Founder): Missionary, educator and theologian who birthed the vision.
  • Dr. (Mrs.) Patricia Hulsey (Worldwide President): Continues the vision across 120+ nations.
