About HIBIAFRICA Directorate

HIBIAFRICA serves as the Continental Headquarters of Harvestime International Network (USA). Its divine mandate is “Training and Equipping Labourers for Worldwide Spiritual Harvest” (Matthew 9:37–38). The African Directorate is located in Osogbo, Osun State, Nigeria, overseeing national chapters and partner ministries across Africa.
