Admissions

Follow the steps to begin your journey at HIBI Africa:

  1. Choose a program level that matches your calling and prior qualifications.
  2. Prepare transcripts/credentials and a passport photograph.
  3. Fill the application form below (attach your documents in your email app after it opens).
  4. Receive admission guidance from our office or your National Chapter Director.

Online Application Form (Email Submission)

Your email app will open with details pre-filled to admissions@hibiafrica.org. Please attach your transcripts & passport photo before sending.

Policies (Snapshot)

  • Assessment model: 40% Continuous Assessment + 60% Final Exams.
  • Study manuals are required each semester.
  • Languages: English, French, Yoruba (selected materials are bilingual).
